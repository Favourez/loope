Emergency Response Guidelines

🚨 CRITICAL: Call Emergency Services First
In any life-threatening emergency, ALWAYS call Cameroon emergency services first:
  • 🔥 Fire Rescue: 118
  • 👮 Police: 117
  • 🚑 Ambulance: 119
When to Use This App:
  • ✅ Reporting non-life-threatening fire incidents
  • ✅ Learning first aid procedures
  • ✅ Receiving emergency alerts in your area
  • ✅ Finding nearby emergency resources
  • ✅ Sharing emergency information with others
When NOT to Rely Solely on This App:
  • ❌ Active house fires with people trapped
  • ❌ Medical emergencies requiring immediate attention
  • ❌ Any situation where seconds count
  • ❌ When you need immediate professional emergency response

First Aid Guidelines

Before Providing First Aid:
  1. Ensure Scene Safety: Make sure the area is safe for you and the victim
  2. Call for Help: Call emergency services if the situation is serious
  3. Get Consent: Ask permission before helping a conscious person
  4. Use Universal Precautions: Protect yourself from bloodborne pathogens
Important Reminders:
  • 🩹 These guides are for educational purposes only
  • 📚 Consider taking a certified first aid course
  • 🔄 Practice techniques regularly to maintain skills
  • 📱 Keep emergency numbers easily accessible
  • 🧤 Always use protective equipment when available
Disclaimer: First aid information in this app is not a substitute for professional medical training or emergency medical services.

Emergency Reporting Guidelines

How to Report Effectively:
  1. Be Accurate: Provide precise location information
  2. Be Clear: Describe the emergency clearly and concisely
  3. Be Honest: Only report real emergencies
  4. Be Available: Stay available for follow-up questions
Information to Include:
  • 📍 Exact location (address, landmarks, GPS coordinates)
  • 🔥 Type and size of fire or emergency
  • 👥 Number of people involved or at risk
  • 🚧 Any hazards or obstacles for responders
  • 📞 Your contact information
Note: False emergency reports are illegal and can result in serious consequences, including fines and criminal charges.
