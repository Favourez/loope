Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection are our top priorities

Last updated: December 2024
Back to Welcome

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better emergency response services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, emergency contacts
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for emergency reporting and alerts
  • Emergency Reports: Details of reported emergencies for response coordination
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns to improve functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for emergency response purposes:

  • Sending emergency alerts relevant to your location
  • Coordinating emergency response services
  • Providing first aid guidance and resources
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Communicating important safety information

3. Information Sharing

We share information only when necessary for emergency response:

  • Emergency Services: Location and emergency details during active incidents
  • Local Authorities: Aggregated data for emergency planning (anonymized)
  • Healthcare Providers: Medical information during medical emergencies (with consent)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order
Important: We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with regular security audits
  • Access controls limiting who can view your information
  • Regular backups to prevent data loss
  • Compliance with industry security standards

5. Location Privacy

Location data is handled with special care:

  • Location is only accessed when you use emergency features
  • You can disable location services at any time
  • Location history is automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Precise location is only shared during active emergencies

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-emergency communications

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Emergency reports: 7 years (for analysis and improvement)
  • Location data: 30 days (for alert relevance)
  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • Usage analytics: 2 years (anonymized)

8. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

If you're using this app outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@emergencyapp.com
  • Phone: 1-800-PRIVACY
  • Mail: Emergency Response App, Privacy Office, 123 Safety Street, Emergency City, EC 12345
Our Commitment

We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing life-saving emergency services. Your trust is essential to our mission of keeping communities safe.
